## Introduction

**Pigsty** <span className="text-fd-muted-foreground text-sm">(/ˈpɪɡ staɪ/)</span> is a battery-included, local-first, open-source & free <span className="text-blue-500 font-bold">PostgreSQL Distribution</span> based on **bare Linux**.
